About Us

Devly's was initiated by the famous Amaradisi hotel which was in business for the last 75 years, and was passed onto her daughter who is also a which in culinary art grabbing gold medal all over the world for her talent is taken, tasted etc. her Husband who is also a corporate chef in Canada managing 10 hotels and also a perfectionist all-rounder. Both have teamed together to open devly's restaurant which is unique in taste and the restaurant tastefully presented. Their motive is not to complicate the looking method but to keep it simple and tasteful without any additives. We strongly believe in customer relation and make them feel homely. We both who were in Canada for the last 22 years achieved very much experience in looking and Conquered gold medal in Germany, Vancouver, Toronto, Singapore. Finally decided to come to our motherland and give a bit of our talent to our Sri Lankans.
